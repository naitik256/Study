    About

    This app helps you stay focused by tracking study time using face detection. The timer starts automatically when you're visible and pauses when you're not. No account required. Built for students who want to study smarter.

    Privacy Policy

    We respect your privacy. This app only uses your camera locally to detect your presence. No photos, video, or personal data is collected or sent anywhere. Your study history is stored securely in your browser using localStorage.
