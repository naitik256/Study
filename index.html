    About

    This app helps you stay focused by tracking study time using face detection. The timer starts automatically when you're visible and pauses when you're not. No login required.

    Privacy Policy

    This app uses your camera locally to detect your presence. No photos or personal data are recorded or sent anywhere. Your study time is stored securely in your browser only.
